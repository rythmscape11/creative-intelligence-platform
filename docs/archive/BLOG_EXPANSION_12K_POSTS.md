**1. Header Section**

  - Framework and methodology

  - Measurement and optimization

**5. Advanced Techniques (300-400 words)**

- Personalization strategies
- Emerging trends

**6. Common Mistakes Section**
- 4-5 major pitfalls
- How to avoid them
- Solutions and alternatives

**7. Tools and Resources**
- Recommended tools
- Platform comparisons
- Resource links

**8. FAQ Section**
- 5-7 common questions
- Direct, concise answers
- Question-based keywords

**9. Action Plan**
- Week-by-week implementation
- Immediate next steps
- Success metrics

**10. Conclusion**
- Summary of key points
- Call-to-action
- Future outlook

### Formatting Guidelines

**Short Paragraphs**:
- 2-3 sentences maximum
- One idea per paragraph
- Ample white space

**Heading Hierarchy**:
- H1: Title (1 per article)
- H2: Main sections (8-12 per article)
- H3: Subsections (15-25 per article)

**Lists and Bullets**:
- Numbered lists for sequential steps
- Bullet points for features/benefits
- Checkboxes for action items

**Callout Boxes**:
- **Pro Tip**: Expert insights
- **Expert Insight**: Industry leader quotes
- **Quick Note**: Important reminders
